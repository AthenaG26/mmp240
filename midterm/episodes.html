Bocchi the Rock

Season 1

E1 - Lonley Rolling Bocchi

Shy loner Hitori practices guitar in hopes of being in a band. But, one day after school, a drummer asks her to temporarily fill in with her group.

E2 - See You Tomorrow

In a bid to make enough money to cover the ticket quota for their next performance, Hitori's bandmates convince her to work with them at Starry.

E3 - Be Right There

After remembering that her band is looking for another member, Hitori tries to work up the courage to approach a popular, multitalented schoolmate.

E4 - Jumping Girl(s)

Now a four-piece, the band starts its activities in earnest; Hitori struggles to write appropriate lyrics for a song that Ryo is composing.

E5 - Flightless Fish

With Ryo's song complete, the girls feel ready to play a show; Nijika asks her sister to book them a slot, but she insists that they audition first.

E6 - Eight Views

Hitori must somehow sell five tickets for her band's upcoming performance. While trying to distribute flyers, she encounters a very hungover woman.

E7 - To Your House

To prepare for the first band's gig, Kita and Nijika design T-shirts at Hitori's house; Hitori tries to be a good host to her friends.

E8 - Bocchi the Rock

A typhoon forces many of the girls' friends and family members to cancel their plans to come to the show; Kessoku Band begins playing to a tiny crowd.

E9 - Enoshima Escar

Wishing her bandmates would invite her out, a forlorn Hitori spends most of her summer vacation doing nothing; Kita suggests a trip to the beach.

E10 - After Dark

Hitori wants to play on stage with the Kessoku Band at her school's festival, but she's torn between her desire for attention and her fear of failure.

E11 - Duodecimal Sunset

After finding Hitori, the girls enjoy the various classroom booths at the cultural festival and rehearse for the next day's performance.

E12 - Morning Light Falls on You

The crowd is thrilled by the first song in Kessoku Band's set, but Hitori breaks a string shortly before her guitar solo in the second tune.
